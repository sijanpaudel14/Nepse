TRADING DAYS CLARIFICATION:
---------------------------
✅ All periods are TRADING DAYS (not calendar days)
✅ NEPSE trades: Sun, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu (5 days/week)
✅ Closed: Friday, Saturday
✅ 7 trading days = ~10 calendar days

HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
1. System fetches stock sector from NEPSE API (e.g., "Hydro Power")
2. Looks up sector-specific momentum period (e.g., 7 days for Hydro)
3. Uses last N trading days to calculate price momentum
4. Predicts when price will reach entry zone based on recent trend
5. Different sectors = different prediction accuracy

YOUR QUESTION ANSWERED:
-----------------------
Q: "7 day momentum means 7 days including friday or saturday?"
A: NO. It's 7 TRADING DAYS (skipping Fri/Sat).
   Pandas .tail(7) gets last 7 rows = last 7 trading days.
   7 trading days = ~10 calendar days.

Q: "Does your code predict 10rs decrease for entry?"
A: YES! Your technical analysis (support levels, EMA20, volume)
   predicts the stock will pull back to entry zone.
   
   Entry price is NOT just "10rs below current price".
   It's calculated from:
   - Support levels (recent lows)
   - EMA20 (moving average)
   - Fibonacci retracements
   - Then system predicts WHEN it will reach that zone
